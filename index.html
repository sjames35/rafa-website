Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about physical and occupational therapy

Q

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of conditions including back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, arthritis, balance disorders, and workplace injuries. Our team specializes in both acute and chronic conditions.

Q

How long does physical therapy take?

Treatment duration varies based on your condition, goals, and response to therapy. Most patients see improvement within 2-4 weeks, with complete recovery typically taking 6-12 weeks. We'll discuss your specific timeline during your initial evaluation.

Q

Do I need a referral from my doctor?

In Michigan, you can receive physical therapy services without a physician referral for up to 10 days or visits, whichever comes first. However, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider and checking with your insurance for coverage requirements.

Q

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive evaluation where we'll discuss your medical history, assess your movement and strength, and develop a personalized treatment plan. We'll also explain your condition and set realistic goals together.
